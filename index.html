Organic Millet Noodles

  • Description

  • Ingredients

  • Cooking Instructions
Millet Noodles made from our specialty millets instead of wheat. In addition to being gluten free, millet is some of the most nutritious and easily digestible grains.

Millet Noodles has a delicious nutty taste and is a good source of fiber and protein. Ready to eat in just 4 minutes! Add to soup or vegetables for a quick bowl of flavorful and nourishing noodles any time.
  •  Oil-Free

  • No Additives

Ingredients: Organic Millet Flour

4 servings per container 

Serving size 1/2 piece serving size


Cooks in 4 minutes!

Step 1

Add ramen cake(s) to boiling water. After one minute start separating noodles gently with a fork.
Cook for 3-4 minutes or until noodles are just soft.

Step 2

Add the flavor pouch included and serve hot!

A unique story

Millet Magic is more than a millet noodle company; we're passionate advocates for sustainability, farmer empowerment, and healthier food. Our journey started with a mission to revolutionize noodle consumption while positively impacting the world.

Sustainability Commitment
Our core is sustainability, nourishing bodies and the planet. We reduce our carbon footprint by sourcing millet locally, employing eco-friendly packaging, conserving water, and enhancing energy efficiency. We hold certifications validating our eco-friendly practices.

Empowering Farmers
Farmers are key to sustainability, and we empower them through fair pricing, training, and community engagement. Together, we build a sustainable future.

Our Millet Noodles
Our delicious, nutritious millet noodles are gluten-free, planet-friendly, and support various dietary preferences. By choosing Millet Magic Noodles, you enjoy a satisfying meal while contributing to a healthier planet and supporting dedicated farmers.

MISSION & VISION
